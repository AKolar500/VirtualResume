Even though most accepted this realistic version, there were some who did not. Among these people was the One. The One was a human being who possessed an anomale inherent in the Matrix that was derived from the Source. This anomale, if left unchecked, would threaten the existence of the Matrix by representing the very nature of the Matrix: choice.

The choice would be presented to the One before the fall of Zion (the city that humans established after they were freed from the Matrix), and the choice would be between the function of the One and the feelings of the One. The difficulty of this choice would depend on the One's attatchment to humanity.
