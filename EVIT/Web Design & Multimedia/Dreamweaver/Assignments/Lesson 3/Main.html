Question: Why Do They Exist?

Answer: Simply to establish a single location where complicated operations and simulations can be performed efficiently and thoroughly.

 

Question: Who Is In Charge?

Answer: Normally, a chosen antagonist from that specific universe is in command of the designated base, but when an outranking member from a different universe enters the base, control of the base shifts to that member.

 

Question: If There Is More Than One Base In A Universe, Who Is In Charge Of All Of Them?

Answer: If there is more than one base in a universe, there is no one being who is in control of all of them.

 

Question: If There Is No One In Charge Of All Bases In A Universe, What Happens If There Is Conflict Between Them?

Answer: If there is conflict, there are a few steps that must be taken to resolve the issue.
