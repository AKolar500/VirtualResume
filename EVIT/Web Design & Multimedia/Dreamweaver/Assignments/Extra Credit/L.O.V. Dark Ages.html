L.O.V. Dark Ages

 

First Dark Age: Members that were once skillful and tactful became reckless and unorganized. Wars broke out between branches of the organization, as well as their original adversaries. Some members secretly continued their devotion to the survival of the organization, though most of them used violence to gain dimensional territory. In the final days of the L.O.V.'s First Dark Age, the organization had mostly collapsed, and former members had gone into hiding.
