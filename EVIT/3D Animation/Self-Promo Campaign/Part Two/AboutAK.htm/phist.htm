My Personal Experiences

Many events have occurred to me over the years, some good and some bad. Some of the good things include: an IQ test at age 4, hooking a sea lion, making powerful friends and acquaintances, collecting things, and more.

When I was 4 years old, I displayed terrific memorial skills when I recited a song that I had heard once the day before word for word, tone for tone. My parents were impressed, and (though I do not know which one, or even if both of them did it) an unidentified party enrolled me in a program for gifted children. My test results showed that at the time, I had the intelligence of a child twice my age. Later, I was pulled from the program.

I tried to keep hold of the rod, but the force was too strong for my pre-adolescent body, so I requested assisstance from my father.
