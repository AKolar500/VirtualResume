Welcome to the Institute of Energy

The Institute of Energy is a not for profit enterprise organized for
the purpose of Educational, Literary and Scientific pursuits in the
energy world.

The corporation has established, operates and conducts the affairs of an Energy Museum, and it fosters, encourages, educates and influences others in energy exploration, energy production, energy distribution, energy sustainability, energy conservation, environmental leadership, and distributed energy to meet our nation’s energy, environmental, transportation, agricultural, energy diversification, energy infrastructure, system reliability and global economic goals.

It supports the energy industry by providing policy statements on energy related issues, including regulatory, legislation, congressional awareness/acceptance, environmental, transportation, and other critical activities. It provides accurate and timely information to policy makers, the public and other constituencies to promote acceptance and recognition of the energy industry’s role in providing safe, secure, reliable, economical, and environmentally conscious energy resources.

IE corporate nature is structured to provide educational, scientific energy research, energy museum, energy policy development, advocacy on energy conservation, sustainability, renewable energy, and other activities as authorized under the non-profit status to advance the cause of energy.
